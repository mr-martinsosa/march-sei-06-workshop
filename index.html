We are all about helping and saving the polar bears of planet earth.

"Save the polar bears" works with world leaders for conservation of polar bears.
We know their habits, ecology, and their threats.

Polar bears live in the Artic Circle. The weather is generally very cold here. To protect against this cold polar bears have a thick coat of fur on top of a heavy layer of fat.Although polar bears usually look white ther fur is actually clear and hollow. The absorption of light gives the polar the appearence of white fur.Although polar bears appear to be freindly deadly wild animals. A polar bears deit consists mainly of seals and walarus.

Why are they endangered? How we can help them?

The polar bears are endangered because climate change. Global warming is causing the ice caps in north and south pole to melt.
This is causing these ice dwelling bears to lose their natural habitat. Also their food source is shrinking because of this.
